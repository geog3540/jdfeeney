James Feeney '27 JD Candidate with a GIS Interest

I am currently a senior at The University of Iowa. In less than a month, I will be graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Criminology, Law and Justice from Iowa’s College of Liberal Arts and Sciences, along with a certificate in Geographic Information Systems and a minor in Rhetoric and Persuasion. My philosophy of learning stems from not wanting to just know “what is a thing”, but “why is a thing the way that it is?” In the past, I’ve completed projects on the rise of sexist and harmful rhetoric in the infamous Amber Heard-Johnny Depp trial, long term drought effects on the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index, and I am currently developing a tool for a local elementary school to geographically log and share their field trip experiences. In the fall, I am enrolling in Iowa’s College of Law, and am hoping to one day go into either Environmental Law or Criminal Law.
